::Bootstrap
one command. the rest follows.
on a fresh Fedora 43 machine:
$ curl -fsSL https://joinrabble.world/bootstrap.sh | bash

This clones the Grimoire, which pulls the rest of the Collective and runs the Ansible playbook. Requires a live Fedora 43 install. Review the script before running.
